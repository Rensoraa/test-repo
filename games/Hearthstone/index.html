Hearthstone

Hearthstone is a digital collectible card game developed and published by Blizzard Entertainment. Players build decks and compete against each other using a variety of cards representing spells, creatures, and abilities. The game features a unique mana system, allowing players to play cards of increasing power as the game progresses. With its strategic gameplay and rich lore, Hearthstone has become a staple in the world of online card games.
