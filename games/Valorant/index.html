Valorant

Valorant is a free-to-play first-person shooter developed and published by Riot Games.
It was released on June 2, 2020, and has since gained a massive player base.
The game features a 5v5 format, where players assume the roles of "agents," each with unique abilities. The objective is to plant or defuse a bomb, known as the "spike," while eliminating the opposing team.
